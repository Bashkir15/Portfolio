What You Can Expect From Me

I am a creative professional with more than 3 years experience creating high-scale, performant web and mobile applications. Creating seamless applications is my passion. Working with me means that you will have your opinion heard, consulting every step of the way, and a partner to help you construct whatever suits your needs to help your business or project succeed

User Experience & User Interface Design

I love engineering website and app interfaces that are as user-friendly as possible. The first thing I always do is put together a list of objectives for a specific page. These vary drastically depending on your website, but can as simple as the completion of a form, calling a telephone number or downloading a resource

Every industry is different and this affects the way a user interacts with your website. I go over every detail to turn these objectives into a visually impressive design. Of course, the goal is to keep the user moving through your website until they've found everything they need to know, and they get in touch

Development & UX

Designing something to look amazing is useless without the development skill to make it come to life

On the Frontend I am an expert using the most up-to-date tools to achieve a flawless user experience along with a lightning fast load time and a fully response, mobile/touch-friendly interface fit with beautiful animations and effects. After all, these are major Search Engine ranking factors and may be the difference between a visitor and a customer

Once your website or application has launched, it'll need to stay up to date. Whether it's adding a new blog post, checking payments, or changing the data I make this as simple as possible. I engineer the backend of your website to be as secure and scalable as possible. No data-structure gets created that isn't the result of a well-thought out process

Support & Future Development

Whether you run a large eCommerce website, have a live booking system or manage a small brochure website it's important that it keeps up with the latest trends.

I will guide you through changes you can make to improve user experience, enhance a journey and add/change functionality and provide the best product to your users

I use high-profile analytics tool to monitor your site's traffic and areas of interest. These hot-and-cold spots help to produce a wider digital marketing strategy. My goal is to keep your product up-to-date, secure, fast and reliable, whilst constantly improving conversion rates and search engine rankings
